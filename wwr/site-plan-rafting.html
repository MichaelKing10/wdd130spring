Overview

Purpose

The Purpose of this website is to create awareness about our business, and to help people find information that would help them plan, book and enjoy their vacations, breaks, getaways and holidays in a nature-themed enviroment. It is also a means to build revenue for the business so as to provide more customer-friendly services.

Audience

The target audience/clients would be families who rarely have time together and would love to have some adventurous bonding time outside the home, the Business class citizens, who once in a while, would like to enjoy a relaxing but adventurous holiday; Students looking to wind down after gruelling semesters and would love an affordable adventure; couples who would love to spend time together, as well as individuals just wanting to enjoy water and nature.
