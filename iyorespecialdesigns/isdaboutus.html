About Iyore Special

Bringing out the Beauty and Excitement in Every Woman.

Iyore Special Designs, established in 2018, has been an outstanding organization geared towards changing the status quo of women fashion. With a blend of modern and antique designs, we have continued to deliver on the grandest level, at every request.

Iyore Special, full name, "Joy Iyore Igbineweka", is a native of Benin, Nigeria. She is the 3rd of six children from her parents. Iyore was born and brought up in Benin, but moved to Lagos, the most industrious city in Nigeria, where she had her high school education. She has been a lover of fashion and clothing designs which fueled her passion for fashion design. She determined to one day be her own boss in the fashion industry.

Iyore's journey towards her dream began after her return from mission. Despite being from humble backgrounds, her determination made her take up different types of jobs to pay for her training. She worked as a sales representative at a furniture enterprise, as well as for other organizations that had some relationship with designs.

Within 2 years, she was able to save up to 70% of the needed fees for her training. She immediately applied to a fashion training organization where she took her lessons. As a dedicated and creative-minded individual, she quickly caught the eye of a lot of fashionistas, who predicted she would have a great future. This made her boss at the time have more confidence and trust in her. She, in no time, became a leader among the apprentices.

More Than Just Clothing!!

Iyore Special, has churned out grand and exciting designs of clothings of different exquisite styles and designs which continues to turn heads. Her dedication to make every woman in her dress stand out has helped her continue to create and produce eye-catching designs which always become a talk of the town. Due to her success, she has opened her doors for other ladies who would want to learn the art and craft of the fashion design business. Despite this level os success, she continues to be humble, dedicated, creative and reliable.
