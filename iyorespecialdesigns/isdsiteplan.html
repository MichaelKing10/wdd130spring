Overview

Purpose

So, there’s that set of clothes you’d like to sew, but you’re at a crossroad. Last one didn’t work out well with your previous designer, and you don’t want a repeat. You really crave a splendid job. That’s what We do… At “Iyore Special Designs”, we give you the best service by bringing to life, your desired designs. We make you look elegant for every occasion. You can count on us to make you look fantastic, and on time too. Want more details about us, check our website.

Audience

Our Audience are mostly women, ladies and teenage girls, of all body shapes, sizes, heights and preferences. Also, we would be dealing with women from all walks of life, be it in the medical, law, engineering, social or economic field. This includes those who are socialites as well as the reserved.
We also would also consider infants, toddlers and adolescent in all of our designs to ensure that they are not left out. Our brand also considers the financial capability of each class of women, and work around this information to ennsure there's provision for them all.
